School students have the whole world of opportunities ahead of them. From Scientists to Engineers to Chartered Accountants to Entrepreneurs to Artists, they can be anything they desire. However, the tremendous amount of options available to them makes the process of choosing a course stream, college and eventually a career more difficult and more confusing.

Absence of proper career counselling in schools, resulted in many students making wrong career choices and then regretting for the rest of their lives.

We furnish you with all the info regarding various courses and colleges in Kerala. Our main features are Assessment test and a Personality Test for the school students which will help those young ones get a good understanding of themselves and advise them on the career path that best suits them.
